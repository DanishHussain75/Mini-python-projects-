Instructor Profile:

He has completed his MS(CS) Degree in 2004 from M.A.J.U Islamabad. Over the period of 20 years in teaching, he has also served in various administrative positions as Head of Department (BS), In charge Final Year Project, Director of Student Affairs, In charge of Sports, Member of the University Disciplinary Committee, Program Coordinator, Event Organizer, and more.

During his career, he has served in various reputable organizations, including Punjab Group of Colleges (PGC) and University of Central Punjab (UCP). He joined FUUAST Islamabad as a permanent faculty member in 2009 as a Lecturer (Computer Science) and has taught courses such as Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Software Project Management (SPM), Software Engineering, Object-Oriented Software Engineering, and Final Year Project.
